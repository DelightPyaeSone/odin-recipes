Shan Noodles

Ingredients

  • 200g rice noodles
  • 100g minced chicken or pork
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp fish sauce
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • Cooking oil
  • Chili flakes
  • Spring onions for garnish

Directions

  1. Cook rice noodles and set aside.
  2. Saut√© garlic and tomatoes in oil; add meat and cook through.
  3. Add soy sauce, fish sauce, and sugar; stir well.
  4. Serve sauce over noodles and top with chili and spring onions.
