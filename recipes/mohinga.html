Mohinga (Fish Noodle Soup)

Home

Ingredients

  • 200g rice noodles
  • 1 fish fillet (catfish or tilapia)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 lemongrass stalk, chopped
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 2 boiled eggs (optional)
  • Banana stem or cabbage slices
  • Water, salt, and oil

Directions

  1. Boil fish with lemongrass, turmeric, and salt.
  2. Debone and mash fish; reserve broth.
  3. Sauté onion and garlic, then add fish and broth.
  4. Simmer with fish sauce; add noodles and garnish with egg and cabbage.
