Tea Leaf Salad (Lahpet Thoke)

Home

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp fermented tea leaves
  • 1/4 cup chopped cabbage
  • 2 tbsp fried peas
  • 1 tbsp roasted peanuts
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds
  • 2 garlic cloves, fried
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 green chili, sliced
  • 1 tsp lime juice
  • Salt to taste

Directions

  1. Mix all dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Add tea leaves, lime juice, and salt.
  3. Toss everything together and serve fresh.
