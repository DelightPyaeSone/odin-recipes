Ohn No Khao Swe (Coconut Noodles)

Home

Ingredients

  • 200g egg noodles
  • 200ml coconut milk
  • 150g chicken breast, sliced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tbsp chickpea flour
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp fish sauce
  • Boiled eggs, lime, and coriander for garnish

Directions

  1. Saut√© garlic, onion, turmeric in oil; add chicken and cook.
  2. Mix chickpea flour with water and add to pan.
  3. Pour in coconut milk and fish sauce; simmer.
  4. Serve over noodles with boiled eggs and lime wedges.
